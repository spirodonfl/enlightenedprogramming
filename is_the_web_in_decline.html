I am not the only one who believes that the web portion of the tech industry is, overall, in decline. A dramatic, precipitous one, which might be an exhaduration on my part but on decline nonetheless.

Some other articles discussing the decline of the web are below. You'll not some are almost a decade old.

  • Website Obesity - 2015

The issue with such articles and opinions is that they tend to lack concrete data. You can pick and choose several websites, take some screenshots of metrics, explore the data of those website, but these are mostly opinions wrapped in anecdotes.

This does not mean the arguments are wrong. It just means they are not provable en masse.
